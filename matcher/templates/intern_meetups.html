University Programs presents Intern Meetups, a program to help you get to know other interns through casual 1:1 meetups.

Meet new friends, grow your network, and form meaningful connections with interns from across the company.

Pairings happen every week – you can opt in or out based on your availability. Just join #intern-meetups to participate.

Join #intern-meetups

How does the program work?

After joining the Slack channel, you’ll receive a message at the start of the next pairing round asking for an introduction.

After introducing yourself, you’ll receive your first pairing.

Message the intern you pair with to meet up!

For following rounds, you’ll be asked if you want to be paired with someone new this week.

Pairing rounds start the first day of each week during the intern season. If at any point you want to leave the program, simply leave the Slack channel.

Why Intern Meetups?

The goal of Intern Meetups is to to help you make new friends and grow your network at PayPal.

It can be difficult to get to know other interns virtually. Participating in Intern Meetups gives you more familiar faces and great connections that can be harder to form on a large virtual meeting.

1:1 meetups also help you gain insight into different parts of the company. You might even find someone you have a lot in common with who you wouldn’t have met otherwise!
